It is important for people to be able to protect themselves and those they care about, but it is also important to distinguish between self-defense and needless aggressiveness. Men should seek to develop a positive sense of self-esteem and assertiveness, as well as learn to regulate their emotions and communicate effectively. The goal should be to make the world a safer and more just place.

HOW TO INCRESE YOUR DANGER

Learn self-defense

In potentially harmful situations, it's crucial to know how to defend both oneself and other people. Think about enrolling in martial arts training or picking up some fundamental self-defense skills.

Practice good communication skills

Conflicts may be avoided from increasing by using effective communication to assist diffuse potentially explosive circumstances. Learn to talk assertively and with respect while paying close attention.
